PASSWORD RESET POLICY

Users can reset their password by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

Steps:
1. Enter your registered email address.
2. Check your email for a password reset link.
3. Click the link and create a new password.
4. Login using the new password.

Password Requirements:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

Reset links expire after 30 minutes.
